# Mobile Shop POS & Repair Management System

A web-based **Mobile Shop Point of Sale (POS) and Repair Management System** designed to help mobile shop owners manage daily business operations such as product sales, accessories inventory, mobile repair jobs, warranty tracking, and business reports in one place.

---

## Features

- POS Billing System (Sales & Receipt Generation)
- Inventory / Stock Management
- Mobile Phone & Accessories Management
- Repair Job Tracking System
- Repair Receipt & Repair Bill Generation
- Customer Management
- Warranty Tracking
- Sales & Income Reports
- Product Search System
- Low Stock Alerts

---
